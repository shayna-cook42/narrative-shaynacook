Welcome to the Net Access Conference

Net Access is a convening of graphic designers in Washington, D.C for a conference regarding accessility on the internet. From use of color on websites to creating proper alt-text, this conference will discuss the commonly accepted standards of accessibility, and propose ways with audience input for alternate, more accessible standards that designers can easily implement.

Join us at the Hilton in Mt. Vernon from September 28th to September 29th 2017. See the schedule for more information.
