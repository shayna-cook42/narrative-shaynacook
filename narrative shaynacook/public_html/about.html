About

This conference was formed by a group of like-minded people concerned about the lack of attention paid to accessibility on the web. While the W3C guidelines exist, we believe that there are better ways to do things to enable and enhance the user experience for everyone, regardless of ability. In 2016 we held a similar conference in San Francsico; this year, we decided to bring it to the East Coast.
